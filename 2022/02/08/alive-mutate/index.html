Supported Mutations

Alive-mutate supports a lot of mutations which belongs to 2 categories. We divide them into Simple Mutation and Complex Mutation. Simple Mutation contains updating on instruciton or chaning variable usage which won’t involve utilizing informaiton of dominance tree. However, Complex Mutation requires dominant information for constructing more complicate structures.

Simple Mutation

Simple Mutations are basically intuitive and simple operations. They are easy to be performed and cancled; thus, for efficency consideration, after applying the mutation to the IR module, the mutation would be cancled to get the orginal module instead of copying the entire module and destory.
